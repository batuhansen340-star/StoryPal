## Description (4000 chars max)

StoryPal turns bedtime into an adventure! Create personalized, AI-illustrated storybooks for your children in just minutes.

**How it works:**
1. Choose an age group (3-5, 5-7, or 7-10)
2. Pick a theme (Space, Ocean, Forest, Castle, and more!)
3. Select a character (Luna, Max, Spark the Dragon, and friends)
4. Personalize with your child's name, appearance, and photo
5. Watch as AI writes and illustrates a unique story

**Key Features:**
- 10 enchanting themes and 10 lovable characters
- AI-generated stories tailored to your child's age
- Beautiful watercolor-style illustrations
- Personalize characters with your child's name and look
- 10 languages supported (English, Spanish, French, German, Turkish, and more)
- Narration with multiple voice characters
- Bedtime mode with calming colors and soothing narration
- Save stories to your library and read them anytime
- Export stories as PDF to share with family
- Track reading stats and streaks
- Safe for children — no violence, no scary elements

**Pricing:**
- Free: 2 stories per day
- Monthly: $4.99/month for unlimited stories
- Yearly: $29.99/year (save 50%)
- Lifetime: $59.99 one-time purchase

Every story is unique and age-appropriate. StoryPal uses advanced AI to craft engaging narratives with consistent character illustrations — making your child the hero of every adventure.

Download StoryPal and create magical memories tonight!

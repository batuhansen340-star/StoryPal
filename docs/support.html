Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a story?

Open StoryPal and tap the "Create Story" button on the home screen. Choose a theme (like Space, Ocean, or Forest), pick a character, select your child's age group, and tap "Create." The AI will generate a personalized story with illustrations in about 30 seconds.

How many free stories can I create?

Free users can create up to 2 stories per day. This resets every 24 hours. For unlimited stories, you can upgrade to StoryPal Premium from the Settings tab.

What languages are supported?

StoryPal supports 10 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Turkish, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Japanese, and Korean. You can select the language before creating a story.

How do I use Bedtime Mode?

While reading a story, tap the controls icon in the top-right corner and toggle "Bedtime Mode" on. This activates a soothing dark theme with gentle narration and auto-play, perfect for winding down before sleep.

Can I personalize a character with my child's name and photo?

Yes! On the character selection screen, tap "Create Custom" to add your child's name and photo. They'll become the hero of the story. Photos are stored securely on your device.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store. Go to your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → StoryPal to modify or cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Can I save or export stories?

All stories are automatically saved to your Library. Premium subscribers can also export stories as PDF files to share or print.

Is StoryPal safe for children?

Absolutely. All AI-generated content is filtered for child safety. Stories are positive, uplifting, and age-appropriate. Images are generated with strict safety guidelines — no scary, violent, or inappropriate content is ever produced.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps: (1) Make sure you have a stable internet connection, (2) Close and reopen the app, (3) Update to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue persists, contact us using the button below.

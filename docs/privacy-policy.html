StoryPal Privacy Policy

COPPA Compliant

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Overview

StoryPal is an AI-powered children's storybook maker app designed for kids and families. We take children's privacy extremely seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international child privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Child's name (optional): Used only to personalize stories. Stored locally on the device only — never sent to our servers.
  • Story preferences: Theme and character selections. Stored locally on the device.
  • Subscription data: Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not process or store payment information.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Photos or camera data (photos are processed locally and not uploaded)
  • Contacts or address book
  • Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
  • Browsing history

We do not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or analytics that collect personal information from children.

4. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: Used to generate story text. Only the story theme, character name, and age group are sent — no personal child data is transmitted.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscription status. RevenueCat processes anonymous subscription data through Apple and does not collect personal information from children.

5. Data Storage

All user data (names, preferences, generated stories) is stored locally on the device. We do not maintain servers that store personal information. Stories and preferences remain on the user's device and are deleted when the app is uninstalled.

6. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review any data stored by the app (all data is on-device)
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Data Security

Since all personal data is stored locally on the device, it is protected by the device's own security mechanisms (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). API communications use HTTPS encryption.

8. No Advertising

StoryPal does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising networks or tracking technologies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

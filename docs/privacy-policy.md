# StoryPal Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

StoryPal ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered children's storytelling application. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information:** Parent's email address and display name for authentication.

**Child Profile:** First name and age range only. We do NOT collect birthdates, last names, or any other identifying information about children.

**Photos:** Only when a parent explicitly uploads a photo for character creation. Photos are processed by AI in real-time and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.

**Usage Data:** Number of stories created, language preference, and app settings. We do NOT collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or device advertising identifiers.

## How We Use Information

- To create personalized AI-generated stories
- To save stories in the family's library
- To improve our service

We NEVER sell personal data to third parties. We NEVER display targeted advertising. We NEVER share children's data with advertisers.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
- Parents may review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information at any time
- Parents can delete all data from the Settings screen within the app
- To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@storypal.app

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication (encrypted at rest)
- **OpenAI:** Story and image generation (only story parameters sent, no personal data)
- **ElevenLabs:** Text-to-speech (only story text sent, no personal data)
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription management (future feature)

## Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete all their data, including child profiles and saved stories, from the Settings screen. Account deletion removes all associated data from our systems.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:
- Email: privacy@storypal.app

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately.
